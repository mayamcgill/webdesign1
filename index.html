Dash to Safety 5k Run/Walk

Join us in Seaside, OR on Saturday, May 6th, for a 5k run/walk through Seaside. This Tsunami preparedness awareness run starts at 9:00am at the Cove in Seaside. The route will take you down the Prom, up Broadway to Wahanna, and finish at Seaside Heights where there will be a FREE Safety Fair from 9:30am - 2:00pm.

Date: Saturday May, 6th
Time: Registration opens at 8:00am at Seaside Heights Elementary, Race starts at 9:00am
Location: Park at Seaside Heights Elementary 2000 Spruce Dr. Seaside, and take a shuttle to the Cove where the race starting line.
Cost: $20 per runner (includes T-shirt), $10 for high school students (no T-shirt)

The Learning Ladder is growing!

We are pleased to announce the expansion of the Sunset Empire Park and Recreation District's preschool programming!

We now offer the families of our community a tiered option for our preschool program, afternoon enrichment activities, as well as a full day option.

Our morning program will offer our traditional preschool program, for ages 2.5 years (and fully potty trained) to 5 years. Our afternoon enrichment and extended activities will include arts and crafts, music and movement, science activities and much more!
All classes will have the benefit of multi-age learning and scaffolding, as well as break out sessions, for the 3-year olds, 4-year-olds and our Pre-K students.
